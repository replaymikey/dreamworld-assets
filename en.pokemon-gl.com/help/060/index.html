About using another DS system

Important!

When you use another Nintendo DS™ system to launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection, the Wi-Fi User Information managed by a server for each Nintendo DS system and saved on your Game Card (Friend Codes, battle records in Rating mode, and Rating information) will be updated.
When this Wi-Fi User Information is updated, all the important data, including Friend Codes, battle records of Random Matchups using Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection, Rankings, and Ratings, will be lost.

Wi-Fi User Information can be safely transferred to another Nintendo DS system.
Tap "Options" under "NINTENDO WFC SETTINGS" in the main menu of the Nintendo DS™ or Nintendo DS Lite, or select "Options" under "Internet" for Nintendo DSi™ or Nintendo DSi XL™, then choose "Transfer Nintendo WFC Configuration" to transfer it to another Nintendo DS system.

Go to http://support.nintendo.com for more details.
