About your Game Sync ID

To use the features linked with your Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version saved data in the Pokémon Global Link, you'll need to register your Game Sync ID issued for Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

Then, tuck in a Pokémon by tapping "ONLINE," then tapping "GAME SYNC" on your C-Gear, and log in to the Pokémon Global Link.

To play with the English version of the Pokémon Global Link, you can only register Game Sync IDs for the English version of Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version. You can't register Game Sync IDs for other versions of Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version.

Check your Game Sync ID

Your Game Sync ID will be issued when you go to the main menu and select "GAME SYNC SETTINGS," or open your C-Gear, tap "ONLINE," then tap "GAME SYNC" to tuck in a Pokémon for the first time through Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection service with your copy of Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

*You'll receive your C-Gear in the game once you've reached Striaton City in your adventure in Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

Please note

*You won't be able to take part in all of the features if you have never tucked in a Pokémon with Game Sync, because your saved data from Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version won't have been sent to the Pokémon Global Link.
If you registered your Game Sync ID when you signed up for the Pokémon Global Link, but haven't tucked in a Pokémon, you'll be temporarily registered. Please tuck in a Pokémon with Game Sync before you log in to the Pokémon Global Link.
If you register your Game Sync ID through the Profile page, you'll need to have your Game Sync ID that you've already used to tuck in a Pokémon with Game Sync.
You may not be able to register an additional Game Sync ID while you are temporarily registered.
*Please remember that you can't change or delete your Game Sync ID after you've registered.

*The Game Sync ID is a combination of ten letters and numbers.
Note that the numbers 0 and 1 and the letters O and I are excluded.
Please enter 8/B, Y/V, O/D, and A/R carefully, since they may look similar to each other.
