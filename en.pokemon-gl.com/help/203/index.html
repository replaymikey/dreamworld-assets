Privacy settings

About privacy settings

Manage your PGL privacy settings on your Pokemon.com My Profile page. To view and change your settings, please visit Pokemon.com, log into your Pokémon Trainer Club account, and click the Edit Profile tab.
Visit Pokemon.com now: http://www.pokemon.com


You can select privacy settings for your Profile, Game Pal Roster, and Dream Pal Roster from the following three types: "Everyone," "Just Friends," and "Nobody."
The default setting is "Nobody," so please click the respective button to choose one of the other settings.
*If you want to have Dream Pals, you'll need to set your privacy settings in your Profile to "Everyone."

About your Profile

You can decide who can see your Profile by changing your Profile's privacy settings.
In the Pokémon Dream World, you can visit other users' homes, but this setting reveals whose homes you can visit.
When two people have different privacy settings, the stricter setting is applied to both.

Everyone

Everyone who uses the Pokémon Global Link can see your Profile.
You can interact with Dream Pals, such as by visiting their homes in the Pokémon Dream World. Home of (Trainer Name) will be randomly displayed on your Dream Pal map, and you can visit that Trainer's home.
You can send E-Z Mail to Game Pals and Dream Pals.

Just Friends

Only Friends among Game Pals can see your Profile.
You cannot interact with other users in the Pokémon Dream World.
You can send E-Z Mail only to Friends among Game Pals.

Nobody

Nobody can see your Profile.
You cannot interact with other users in the Pokémon Dream World.
You cannot send E-Z Mail.
This is the default setting. If your setting is "Nobody," you won't be able to communicate with other users in the Pokémon Global Link.

About the Game Pal Roster and the Dream Pal Roster

This setting enables you to control who you share your Game Pal Roster and Dream Pal Roster with.

Everyone

Everyone can see your Game Pal Roster and Dream Pal Roster.

Just Friends

Friends can see your Game Pal Roster only.

Nobody

Nobody can see your Game Pal Roster or Dream Pal Roster.
Your Profile's privacy settings reflect what is open in your Game Pal Roster and Dream Pal Roster.
