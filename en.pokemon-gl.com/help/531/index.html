Signing up for a competition

Entry through the GBU

Select a competition marked as "Accepting" on the Wi-Fi Competition page in the Pokémon Global Union. Read the Competition Info, Notes and Regulations, and Game Settings and Operation sections carefully. Then, click "Go to Entry Page." Cick the check box next to "I agree to the Notes and Regulations," and click the Entry button. This completes your entry in the competition.

Downloading your Digital Player ID

Select "BATTLE COMPETITION" under the main menu of Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version. Select "To the Wi-Fi Competition" and choose "Participate" when asked "Will you participate in the Wi-Fi Competition?"
Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection will be launched for your Digital Player ID.

Selecting Pokémon for your Battle Box

Register Pokémon in your Battle Box for the competition.
Please note that once Pokémon in your Battle Box are registered, you will not be able to alter your Battle Box.
Once you register the Battle Box, the admission process is complete.

Please note

*Once the competition has started, it's too late to register. Make sure to register in time.
*You won't be able to participate in competitions if you change the order of moves of the Pokémon in your Battle Box.
