About the demo version

All of the features of the Pokémon Dream World are available only to people who have purchased Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version. Please launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection with your copy of Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version to receive your Game Sync ID, and enter it under "Game Registration" in your Profile at the Pokémon Global Link.

You can still play the demo version of the Pokémon Dream World before purchasing Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version, and enjoy a limited selection of features of the Pokémon Dream World. Although you won't be able to communicate with Dream Pals, visit Dream Pals' homes, or exchange items or Berries through Share Shelves, you're welcome to explore the Island of Dreams and grow Berries in your garden.
After obtaining a Game Sync ID in Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version, you can switch your demo version to a registered version by registering your Game Sync ID at your Profile page.
