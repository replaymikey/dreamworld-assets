Leave the Pokémon Dream World

Click the "Leave the Pokémon Dream World" icon in the upper-right corner of the Pokémon Dream World screen. Then turn on the C-Gear in your copy of Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version, tap "ONLINE," and tap "GAME SYNC" to launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection to wake up your sleeping Pokémon.

When you click the "Leave the Pokémon Dream World" icon in the upper-right corner, or when your time runs out, choose "Exit and prepare to wake," or "Exit and continue sleeping." To send Pokémon and/or items to Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version, please choose "Exit and prepare to wake."

Please note

You can't wake up your Pokémon with Game Sync while playing in the Pokémon Dream World. Please leave the Pokémon Dream World by selecting the button "Exit and prepare to wake up" before you try to wake up your Pokémon with Game Sync.
