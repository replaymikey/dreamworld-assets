How to become Game Pals

How two people become Game Pals varies:

Friend
Exchange Friend Codes in Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.
Trade Pal
Trade Pokémon in Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version through trade services such as the Global Trade Station.

Friends

To become Friends in the Pokémon Global Link, you need to launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection in Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

(1) When you launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection in Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version, your Friend Code will be issued in the Pal Pad.

(2) Tap "IR," then "FRIEND CODE" on the C-Gear to exchange Friend Codes via an Infrared Connection.

(3) When both of you have tucked in a Pokémon and have registered a Game Sync ID in the Pokémon Global Link, you can become Friends in the Pokémon Global Link.


*You may register up to 32 Friend Codes.

*If you aren't connected to Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection, the Friend Codes you exchange are temporary and you won't be displayed as Friends in the Pokémon Global Link. Please launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection and obtain formal Friend Codes. Then exchange Friend Codes again.

*If you register Friend Codes manually by using the Pal Pad in the Key Items Case in your Bag, you won't be displayed as Friends in the Pokémon Global Link. In this case, if both of you launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection and go to the Wi-Fi Club, you can register your Friend Codes officially.

*If you use a Nintendo DS™ system different from the one you used to launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection, the Pal Pad information will be erased when you launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection with the Nintendo DS system.

Trade Pals

To become Trade Pals in the Pokémon Global Link, you need to trade Pokémon with another user. There are five ways to trade Pokémon in Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version:
・IR (Infrared Connection)
・Wi-Fi Club
・Union Room
・GTS
・GTS Negotiations

*Your list of Trade Pals can go up to 100.
*If you use a Nintendo DS™ system different from the one you used to launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection, the Trade Pal information will be erased when you launch Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection with the Nintendo DS system.
*To become Trade Pals with another user, you both need to register your Game Sync IDs at the Pokémon Global Link, and tuck in at least one Pokémon with Game Sync.
