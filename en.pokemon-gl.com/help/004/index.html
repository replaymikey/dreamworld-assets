Steps before signing up

Introduction

The English version of the Pokémon Global Link is a membership service for members of the website Pokémon Trainer Club. There are no fees for registration or annual membership. To use the Pokémon Global Link, sign up for a Pokémon Trainer Club account.

Registration for the Pokémon Trainer Club

If you don't yet have an account at the Pokémon Trainer Club, click "SIGN UP!" at the Pokémon Global Link, then follow the instructions.

If you already have a Pokémon Trainer Club account, log in to your Trainer Club account from the Pokémon Global Link home page.

Next, sign up for the Pokémon Global Link account. This is a second account, separate from your Trainer Club account.

Sign up for the Pokémon Global Link

You need to register the Game Sync ID from the English version of Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version to take part in all the features linked with your Game Card in the Pokémon Global Link.
Then, tuck in a Pokémon using Game Sync and log in to the English version of the Pokémon Global Link.
Please set up Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection service for your copy of Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

Wireless broadband Internet connection is required for online features. For more information, go to http://support.nintendo.com.

*For Nintendo DSi™ and Nintendo DSi XL™, you'll need to accept the terms of the Nintendo DSi Network Services Agreement in the System Settings.
Please read the Instruction Booklet for details.

To get your Game Sync ID, go to the main menu and select "GAME SYNC SETTINGS," or open your C-Gear, tap "ONLINE," then tap "GAME SYNC" to tuck in a Pokémon for the first time through the Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection service with your copy of Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

*You'll receive your C-Gear in the game once you've reached Striaton City in your adventure in Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

You can register your Game Sync ID after you log in to the English version of the Pokémon Global Link with your Pokémon Trainer Club account.

*At the Pokémon Global Link, you can register your Game Sync ID for one copy of Pokémon Black Version and one copy of Pokémon White Version per Pokémon Trainer Club account.

To play with the English version of the Pokémon Global Link, you can register Game Sync IDs only from the English version of Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version. You can't register Game Sync IDs from other versions of Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version.

Please note

*You won't be able to take part in all of the features if you have never tucked in a Pokémon with Game Sync, because your saved data from Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version won't have been sent to the Pokémon Global Link.
If you registered your Game Sync ID when you signed up for the Pokémon Global Link, but haven't tucked in a Pokémon, you'll be temporarily registered. Please tuck in a Pokémon with Game Sync before you log in to the Pokémon Global Link.
*Please remember that you can't change or delete your Game Sync ID after you've registered.

*The Game Sync ID is a combination of ten letters and numbers.
Note that the numbers 0 and 1 and the letters O and I are excluded.
Please enter 8/B, Y/V, O/D, and A/R carefully, since they may look similar to each other.
