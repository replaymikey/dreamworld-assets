Pokémon GLOBAL LINK

The PGL is a website linked to Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version.

The Pokémon Dream World lets you step into a Pokémon's dream within the Pokémon Global Link.
You can play minigames with Pokémon from other regions and communicate with other players.

You can take Pokémon that became your friends and items you obtained in the Pokémon Dream World to Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version.

In the Global Battle Union, you can check Battle Rankings and Ratings of Trainers all over the world. By checking the Rankings, you can see who's the strongest and what kinds of battles they have had.

Your battle data in Random Matchups (Rating Mode) is also displayed in the Rankings, so you can see how strong you are and enter a competition.

Fun stuff to make Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version even better!

*The Pokémon Global Link is an added feature to enhance Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version.
It does not affect the progress of the game.
