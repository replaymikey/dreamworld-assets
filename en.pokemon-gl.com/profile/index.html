            About Game Sync

            You can check your ID by selecting "GAME SYNC SETTINGS" at the main menu after you've reached a certain point in your adventure in Pokémon Black Version, Pokémon White Version, Pokémon Black Version 2, and Pokémon White Version 2.

            Each Pokémon Trainer Club account can have one Game Sync ID for Pokémon Black Version, Pokémon White Version, Pokémon Black Version 2, and Pokémon White Version 2.

            The Game Sync ID cannot be changed or deleted after it has been registered.
