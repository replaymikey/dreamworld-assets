About garden beds

Your garden has garden beds. You can grow three plants per bed.

Here's how

Press the ↑↓ buttons to move between garden beds.

To grow many Berries

The number of garden beds in your garden may increase as you gain Dream Points. You can grow more Berries when you have more garden beds.
