About Game Pals and Dream Pals

To communicate with other users in the Pokémon Global Link, you must make Game Pals or Dream Pals.

*The Game Pal Roster and Dream Pal Roster are updated once a day.

About Game Pals

Game Pals are users connected in Pokémon Black Version, Pokémon White Version, Pokémon Black Version 2, or Pokémon White Version 2. Game Pals are divided into two categories: Friend and Trade Pal.

Friend
Friends are people who have shared Friend Codes in Pokémon Black Version, Pokémon White Version, Pokémon Black Version 2, or Pokémon White Version 2.

Trade Pal
Trade Pals are people you have traded Pokémon with in Pokémon Black Version, Pokémon White Version, Pokémon Black Version 2, or Pokémon White Version 2 through trade services such as the Global Trade Station.

You can exchange E-Z Mail with Game Pals.
Also, you can visit each other's homes in the Pokémon Dream World.

About Dream Pals

Dream Pals are people you can meet and communicate with in the Pokémon Dream World.
Make a Dream Pal request to become someone's Dream Pal.
Once you two have become Dream Pals, you can visit each other's homes.

*To make Dream Pals, the privacy setting for My Page needs to be set to "Everyone."

Please note

・The default privacy settings in the Pokémon Global Link are set to "Nobody" for My Page and for Game Pal and Dream Pal Rosters.
To communicate with other users, you'll need to change your privacy setting for your My Page. To manage your PGL privacy settings, go to your Pokemon.com Profile page. Log in to your Pokémon Trainer Club account, go to the Edit Profile tab, and set your Pokémon Global Link Preferences to "Everyone." Afterward, when you play in the Pokémon Dream World and visit the homes of other users who have also set their privacy setting for the Game Pal Roster and Dream Pal Roster to "Everyone," you'll be able to see their Dream Pal maps and communicate with more people.

・Friends and Trade Pals are added to your Game Pal Roster after  you and the other user have both tucked in at least one Pokémon with Game Sync.
