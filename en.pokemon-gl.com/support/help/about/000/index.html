About the Pok√©mon Global Link

The Pok√©mon Global Link

The Pokémon Global Link website links with the saved data on your Pokémon Black Version, Pokémon White Version, Pokémon Black Version 2, and Pokémon White Version 2 Game Cards.

The Pok√©mon Dream World

The Pokémon Dream World is a dream world that unfolds in the Pokémon Global Link. In the Pokémon Dream World, you can redecorate your home or make friends with Pokémon by playing minigames.

Wi-Fi Competitions

Wi-Fi Competitions are battle competitions using Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection. Sign up for a competition in the Pokémon Global Link to participate in the competition with your Game Card.

Reports

Reports show various rankings and other reports based on the play results of players all over the world.

On the Global Trade Station (GTS) tab, you can check the rankings of the most-traded Pokémon and which Pokémon are often traded for a particular Pokémon.

On the Global Battle Union (GBU) tab, you can check battle Rankings and Ratings of players all over the world who competed using Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection. The results are shown by season.

On the Wi-Fi Competitions tab, you can check Rankings in competitions and rankings of Pokémon used by top players.

On the Game Sync World Records tab, you can check rankings that show the characteristics of different places around the world.

Other Features

Customize lets you switch Pokédex designs and C-Gear skins, and add Pokémon Musical shows in your Game Card.

E-Z Mail lets you send mail easily by answering simple questions or selecting words from a list.

When you send E-Z Mail to people abroad, the language of the mail will be changed automatically. This way, the recipient can read the mail in his or her own language.

How to join in the PGL fun

You must register the Game Sync ID issued in your Game Card to use all the fun features of the Pokémon Global Link.

Then, tuck in a Pokémon using Game Sync in your game, and you'll be ready to log in to the Pokémon Global Link.
