Check Rankings of Wi-Fi Competitions

Once Rankings of a Wi-Fi competition are released, go to "Wi-Fi Competitions" in the Pokémon Global Link and check "Competitions Recently Ended."You can check the Rankings there.
Press the "Rankings of Past Competitions" to jump to the Reports page where you can see Rankings and other information for the past competitions. Select a listed compeition to see Ranking of the past competition on the detail screen.

Please see the manual for "Reports" to check information such as Rankings.
