Pokémon GLOBAL LINK

The Pokémon Global Link Is Coming This Spring!

The Pokémon Global Link is a website linked with the Pokémon Black Version and Pokémon White Version games for the Nintendo DS™ system. To use the Pokémon Global Link website, you'll need a Pokémon Trainer Club account. But you don't have to wait until the Pokémon Global Link opens-head to Pokemon.com right now to create an account so you're ready when the site opens!

Pokémon Dream World

The Pokémon Dream World is a wonderful place that your Pokémon can visit in their dreams. In the Pokémon Dream World, you can communicate with other players, find items and Berries, and meet Pokémon that you can bring into your copy of the Pokémon Black Version or Pokémon White Version game!

Global Battle Union

At the Global Battle Union, Trainers can check their match Rankings and Ratings worldwide. See the results of matches you've played in and track how well you're doing in battles against players around the world! If a Nintendo® Wi-Fi Connection* tournament is available, you can participate and put your skills to the test!

*Wireless broadband Internet connection required for online features. For more info, go to support.nintendo.com.
